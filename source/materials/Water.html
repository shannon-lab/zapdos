Water

Material properties of water species

Overview

Water defines the transport and rate coefficients for solvated electrons in water. Water supplies the transport coefficient (i.e., the mobility and diffusion coefficients) for the following species:

  • solvated electrons,

  • H radical,

  • OH ion,

  • HO ion,

  • OH radical,

  • H molecule,

  • O ion,

  • HO ion,

  • HO molecule,

  • HO ion,

  • O molecule,

  • O ion,

  • O radical,

  • HO radical,

  • O molecule, and

  • O ion

Water supplies the rate coefficients for the following reactions:

  • e + HO H + OH

  • 2e + 2HO H + 2OH

  • e + H + HO H + OH

  • e + OH OH

  • e + O + HO 2OH

  • e + HO H + HO

  • e + HO OH + OH

  • e + HO + HO OH + 2OH

  • e + O O

  • e + O O

  • H + HO H + OH

  • 2H H

  • H + OH HO

  • H + OH HO + e

  • H + HO OH + HO

  • H + HO H + OH + HO

  • H + O HO

  • H + HO HO

  • O + HO 2OH

  • O + O O

  • 2OH HO

  • OH + O HO

  • OH + H H + HO

  • OH + OH O + HO

  • OH + HO HO + O

  • OH + O OH + O

  • O + HO OH + OH

  • O + H OH + H

  • O + HO O + HO

  • O + HO O + OH

  • O + O O

  • O + O + HO 2OH + O

  • OH + HO HO + HO

  • OH + HO OH + HO

  • HO + HO HO + OH

  • HO + OH H + OH + HO

  • HO + HO HO + O

  • HO + O HO + HO

commentnote:Citation

The transport and rate coefficients used in this object can be found in Tian and Kushner (2014).

This is fast to determine via a pregenerated node-to-elem map and is robust on conforming meshes. In the optional all_proximate_sides algorithm, primary boundary elements are searched iff they touch that nearest node, even if they are not topologically connected to it.
